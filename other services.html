HORSE HIKING

If your dream is to enjoy a gentle ride on Shauri Lake then this is the break for you! The accommodation is in our lovely guest house or self catered appartments all of which look out over the promenade and Shauri Lake. We offer single, twin, double and family rooms all with en-suite or private bathrooms, some with Lake (supplement to pay). This afternoon you will begin your riding holiday. We suggest that you start with a lesson to enable instructors to assess your level and to allocate you with an appropriate horse.

TOURS

After breakfast this morning you will head back to the stables. You will be riding twice today, you can choose from additional tuition, beach rides or woodland rides (suitable for beginners). We will talk to you about an appropriate programme for your level of experience, most people choose a mixture. All levels are able to ride on the beach, riders will be divided into ability groups on the beach so that everyone is riding at an appropriate level. The stable is located a short hack away from the beach and you will ride through town to get to the beach.

SIGHTSEEING

You will visit Racha, one of the most beautiful parts of Georgia. Here you will explore how it is possible to live in the wild nature. Mravaldzali is one of the highest villages in Europe. People here live in the wild nature. There are lots of historical churches and castles in Racha, you will visit 3 of them during that tour Mravaldzali, Nikortsminda, Barakoni cathedrals. All of them are masterpieces of Georgian architecture. On another hand you will visit cave full of stalactites, stalagmites, and centuries-old ice columns from 8 to 20m and enjoy amazing sightseeing.
